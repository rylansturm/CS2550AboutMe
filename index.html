My Personal Interests

My personal interests center mainly around the two things I've pursued in school. I was seeking a degree in Music Education. Though I haven't found as much time as I'd like lately for this interest, I enjoy playing piano and singing, picking up new instruments, and recording songs either by myself or with friends.

I am unsurprisingly also interested in computer projects. I get a lot of joy out of coding, whether for work, school, or personal projects. I also have a handful of Raspberry Pis and Arduino boards I like to tinker with. My workstation next to me is currently covered in PCBs and switches for a split keyboard that I am soldering together.

Several years ago my sister pestered me to start doing CrossFit with her, and that led me to a whole new interest. I have done a lot of CrossFit over the past 8 years, and even went through the training to coach it. For the past few years I have mainly only done basic strength and conditioning. For the last year I have been coaching two clients remotely to make a little extra money.
